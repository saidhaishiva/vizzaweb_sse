About Shopkeepers Policy

Shopkeeper’s package policy covers all the probable risks/perils faced by shop owners. It offers protection for the property and the interests of the insured in the business venture to cover the loss/damage against financial loss. The policy covers the following

  1. It covers loss or damage to buildings and contents due to fire/lightning/strike/riots/floods/earthquake/burglary and such other perils.
  2. Loss of money due to burglary of cash in safe place or in transit from shop to bank/ATM and bank to shop
  3. Damages caused to glow/neon signs displayed at the shop premises
  4. Financial losses due to fraud/dishonesty of employees

It also offers compensation of liability claims, provides benefit for medical expenses and accidental benefits.
