About Group Health Insurance

The GROUP HEALTH INSURANCE is available for any Group/Association/Institution/Corporate Body provided that the group is not formed for the sake of insurance.

The group health insurance can be availed after deleting domiciliary hospitalization benefit. It can include maternity extension and the preexisting disease cover can also be opted. It can also be made as a floater for every individual member’s family.

A group discount is also available at the same time if it is an existing insured group there will be a loading or discount based on the previous year claim experience. If the group is opting for health insurance for the first time then the group will have a loading or discount on the premium based on the claims experience at the time of renewal of the policy.

There is a discount provision if the group opts for a copay and it is possible to cover the parents also.

A group health insurance can be tailor made to suite the requisites of the group in a cost effective way and a wide coverage can be obtained.
