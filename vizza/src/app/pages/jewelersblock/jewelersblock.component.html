About Jeweller's Block Policy

Jeweller’s block insurance policy is designed to provide comprehensive cover for jewellers and diamantaires. Jewellers block coverage protects against physical loss or damage to precious and semi-precious stones, jewels, jewellery, precious metals, mounts and alloys, packing materials, cash and other business related stocks typical to the jewellery trade, including stocks of others entrusted to the insured or property of an insured entrusted to others at the business premises. The policy also offers protection when such valuables are in transit or under the custody of the insured, employees, directors, sorters, angadias and cutters. In addition to the valuables, covers the furniture, fixtures, office equipments and machines such as air conditioners, used for business purposes by the jeweller. The following perils can be insured.

  1. Theft and Robbery
  2. Shoplifting
  3. Burglary, dacoity and house-breaking,
  4. Fidelity (dishonesty by employees and business partners),
  5. Fire Riot and strike, malicious damage, impact damage, aircraft damage, earthquake, storm, flood and such other perils
  6. Transit with third-party transporter or posts by angadias, registered parcel post, air freight, inland waterways, rail and/or road.
