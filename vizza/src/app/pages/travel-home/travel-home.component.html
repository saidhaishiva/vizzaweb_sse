About Travel Insurance

OVERSEAS TRAVEL INSURANCE

Overseas travel insurance protects a group of people, individual travelling together, individually against any medical and other travel-related issues. This policy offers coverage for emergency medical and health expenses, lost baggage and more. The key areas that is covered under this policy are

  • Cover for emergency medical deportation, evacuation, repatriation
  • Baggage Loss
  • Cover for key documents such as passport etc.
  • Cover against hijack disaster and other catastrophes



NOTE: THE BRIEF WRITE UP YOU FIND IN THIS WEBSITE FOR VARIOUS POLICIES IS ONLY A SYNOPSIS. THE DETAIL EXTENT OF COVERAGE WITH THE TERMS & CONDITIONS WILL BE SENT BY US TO YOUR MAIL ID ON REQUEST TO US. PLEASE MAIL TO CUSTOMERCARE@VIZZAFIN.COM
